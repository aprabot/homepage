The platform

A Multi-Agent Engine for forecasting and optimization

Multi agent environment that monitors the internal and external vectors shaping demand, generating high-confidence forecasts enabling optimal decision making for businesses.

Multi horizon Forecasting

Forecasts for multiple horizons enabling both short term and long term decision making for the businesses

Demand Sensing

Ingest weather, promotions, pricing and other macro signals in near real time to catch shifts weeks before they hit your warehouse.

Scenario Planning

Simulate price changes, supply shocks, or a new launch and watch the ripple across revenue, inventory and service levels instantly.

Inventory Optimization

Predict inventory requirements across every node and channel, ensuring the right stock is available where demand is expected—while minimizing carrying costs and stockouts.

Infrastructure right sizing

Align infrastructure capacity with forecasted demand to reduce bottlenecks, avoid overprovisioning, and improve utilization.

Conversational Intelligence
